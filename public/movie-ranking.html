Best movies of all time according to Sameer Bhattarai

My top three best movies of all time.

3 Idiots

"3 Idiots" is often celebrated as the best movie due to its engaging storyline, perfect blend of humor and serious themes, strong social commentary on education and societal pressures, emotionally resonant characters, stellar performances, memorable dialogues,and universal themes of friendship, ambition, and the pursuit of happiness. Its relatability and entertainment value have made it widely popular among audiences worldwide.

Dilwale Dulhania le jayenge

"DDLJ," which stands for "Dilwale Dulhania Le Jayenge," is indeed a highly acclaimed Bollywood movie known for its entertainment value and romantic storyline. The film has gained iconic status for its portrayal of love, family values, and the theme of following one's heart against all odds. The chemistry between the lead actors, Shah Rukh Khan and Kajol, along with its memorable dialogues, songs, and picturesque locations, contributes to its enduring popularity as a beloved romantic movie in Indian cinema.

Avenger End Game

"Avengers: Endgame" is highly recommended as a must-watch for several reasons. It serves as a monumental culmination of the Marvel Cinematic Universe (MCU), wrapping up storylines from previous films while delivering thrilling action sequences, emotional depth, and satisfying character arcs. The movie's epic scale, stellar performances, and unexpected twists make it a significant and unforgettable cinematic experience for fans of superhero movies and the MCU alike.
